welcome to my website!

hi everyone ✨ i'm 'musing&making' aka kodi :3 thanks for coming by to my corner of the internet. this is basically just a place for me to put all the fun things i care about into one place i created myself (with the help of many many tutorials)

i love early 2000s nostalgia and view indie websites, and coding in general, like a very cool art form!! i feel like i missed all the fun trends and the sillyness that was the early internet by being born basically as it was all happening (04 babies rise up) so learning to code and create my own indie website was a way for me to get in touch with a side of the world i technically was alive for but wasn't really capable of understanding yet.

please take a look around and get to know me !! though this is my first time coding a website since i was sitting in my computer science class in year 8, messing around with making text different colours, so please forgive me if things aren't working up to snuff. i hope to keep improving this page as i learn more about coding and cram more nerdy info into my brain :)
